The players

- Each team has 11 players on the field with substitutes on the sidelines. - While there's no set formation, a common one is 4-4-2 (four defenders, four midfielders, two strikers). - Players can move anywhere on the field, and wingers often switch sides to unsettle the defense. **Common Positions:** - **CDM (Centre Defensive Midfield):** Defensive midfielders sit back when the ball moves forward, known for both playmaking and tackling. - **CAM (Centre Attacking Midfield):** Attacking midfielders in the center, focused on goal-scoring opportunities. - **RWB/LWB (Right and Left Wing Backs):** Defenders who love running up the wing but must quickly return to defend against counter-attacks. - **RW/LW (Right and Left Wing):** Wide attackers in a 4-3-3 setup, running up the wing and either cutting inside or crossing the ball. - **CF (Centre Forward):** Similar to a striker but plays slightly more forward toward the goal, with no fixed area on the field.
